TECHNOLOGY

E. Miroglio has created Rewoollife, combining NewLife recycled polyester fibre from Sinterama with NO mulling merino fine chlorine-free wool. The result is an environmentally freindly, heat-regulating easy care yarn, with the breathability properties and softness of the best merino wool. An eco-friendly yarn, totally made in Europe.
